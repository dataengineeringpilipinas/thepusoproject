Data Deep Dive

Data Deep Dive is an initiative to explore open datasets from data.gov.ph one step at a time. Our goal is to encourage data curiosity and practical insight generation.

  1. Work on the published data in Data Jobs.
  2. Perform data cleaning and wrangling.
  3. Conduct exploratory data analysis.
  4. Create visualizations to highlight key insights.
  5. Write a short summary of your findings and recommendations.
  6. Submit your work to the DEP DataHub.
